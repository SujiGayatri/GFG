<h2><a href="https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/problems/compete-the-skills5807/1?page=4&category=Arrays,Strings&difficulty=Easy&status=unsolved&sortBy=submissions">Compete the skills</a></h2><h3>Difficulty Level : Difficulty: Easy</h3><hr><div class="problems_problem_content__Xm_eO" style="--text-color: var(--problem-text-color);"><p><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 14pt;">A and B, two programmer friends, compete by comparing their skills. They do so by comparing their <span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-weight: bolder; font-family: 'Source Sans 3' !important;">three</span>&nbsp;skills as per their values.</span></p>
<p><span style="box-sizing: border-box; font-size: 14pt;">Skills of A are <strong>a[]</strong> = [a1, a2, a3]<br style="box-sizing: border-box;">Skills of B are <strong>b[] </strong>= [b1, b2, b3]</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">For each index, the one with the higher value earns 1 point. Return an array containing their final scores.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Examples :</strong></span></p>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>a[] = [4, 2, 7], b[] = [5, 6, 3]<br></span><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Output: </strong>[1, 2]<br><strong>Explanation:</strong> Total score of A is 1 and B is 2 because 4 &lt; 5, 2 &lt; 6 and 7 &gt; 3<br></span></pre>
<pre><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Input: </strong>a[] = [4, 2, 7], b[] = [5, 2, 8]
<strong>Output: </strong>[0, 2]<br></span><strong style="font-size: 18px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;">Explanation:</strong><span style="font-size: 18px; font-family: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, 'Segoe UI', Roboto, Oxygen, Ubuntu, Cantarell, 'Open Sans', 'Helvetica Neue', sans-serif;"> 4 &lt; 5, 2 = 2 and 7 &lt; 8</span></pre>
<p><span style="font-size: 18px;"><strong>Constraints :<br></strong>a.size(), b.size() = 3<strong><br></strong></span><span style="font-size: 18px;">1 ≤ a[i], b[i] ≤ 10<sup>9</sup><br></span></p></div><br><p><span style=font-size:18px><strong>Topic Tags : </strong><br><code>Arrays</code>&nbsp;<code>Data Structures</code>&nbsp;
